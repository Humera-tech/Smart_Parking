Unlock the Experience

  • Pre-Booking System

    Allows users to reserve parking slots in advance, ensuring a hassle-free experience, especially during peak hours or events. Users can select time slots, location, and duration beforehand.

  • Group Booking

    Ideal for family outings or group trips involving multiple vehicles. Users can book adjacent slots together by entering the number plates of all vehicles involved to avoid unauthorized access and ensure parking availability.

  • Safe Slot Monitor

    Enhances parking security by actively monitoring booked slots. If another vehicle attempts to park in a reserved slot, the system sends an instant alert to the rightful user, helping prevent misuse.

  • Smart Park Guide

    Solves the common issue of forgetting where the car is parked. Uses augmented reality (AR) to display a marker or path guiding users back to their parked vehicle, based on the stored location.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on SmartPark? ▼

Creating an account is simple! Click on the “Login” button at the top-right corner, then select “Sign Up.” Fill in your details like name, email, and password, and you’re ready to start booking your parking slots securely.

Is the SmartPark system secure? ▼

Yes! All transactions are encrypted and we partner only with verified parking providers and secure payment gateways.

How do I book a parking slot using SmartPark? ▼

Open the app or website, enter your location, select your preferred time slot and parking location, and confirm your booking in just a few clicks.

What if I can't find my car? ▼

No worries! Use the AR Park Finder feature in our app to visually guide you to your vehicle with step-by-step arrows.

Can I book for a group or multiple vehicles? ▼

Absolutely! Use our group booking feature to reserve multiple slots for your team or family members in one go.
